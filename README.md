# 🌍 Climate Impact and Correlation Analyzer

An interactive data visualization platform that explores relationships between key climate indicators such as global temperature, carbon emissions, sea-level rise, and natural disasters.

---

## 🚀 Overview
The Climate Impact and Correlation Analyzer is designed to help users understand complex climate patterns through interactive and intuitive visualizations. By integrating multiple datasets, the platform uncovers correlations and trends that support data-driven decision-making for climate research and policy.

---

## 📊 Features
- 🌡 **Temperature Visualizations**
  - Calendar Heatmaps
  - 3D Globe Representation
  - Choropleth Maps

- 🌍 **Carbon Emissions Analysis**
  - Heatmaps and Bar Charts
  - Time-series Line Graphs

- 🌊 **Sea-Level Rise Analysis**
  - Bar Charts, Scatter Plots, Box Plots

- 🌪 **Natural Disaster Trends**
  - Stacked Bar Charts for disaster frequency

- 🔗 **Correlation Analysis**
  - Heatmaps and Line Plots showing relationships between:
    - Temperature anomalies
    - Carbon emissions
    - Sea-level rise
    - Natural disasters

## 🔍 Key Insights
- Strong positive correlation between carbon emissions and global temperature rise  
- Accelerated sea-level rise linked to increasing temperatures  
- Increase in frequency of natural disasters with temperature anomalies  
- Regional disparities in emissions and climate impact
